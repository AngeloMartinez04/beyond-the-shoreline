The Vision

From Land
Congestion to
Marine Innovation

As coastal cities strain under the weight of rapid urbanization, Subic Bay Floating Business District offers a singular opportunity: to build commerce upon the very waters that once separated people from prosperity.

Beyond the Shoreline is not a concept—it is an engineered reality. A floating district designed with climate resilience as its foundation, smart systems as its nervous system, and the Philippine sea as its stage.

Anchored by biorock, powered by tide and wind, and connected by fiber-optic marine infrastructure, this district redefines where business happens.

FLOATING BUSINESS DISTRICT

Biorock Systems

Electrolytic mineral accretion accelerates coral growth 3–5× faster than natural rates, reinforcing underwater foundations while restoring marine biodiversity beneath the district.

Wave Converters

Oscillating water column arrays capture tidal kinetic energy, converting Subic Bay's consistent swells into clean, continuous baseload power for the entire floating district.

Spiral Axis Wind Turbines

Vertical-axis helical turbines operate silently at low wind speeds, generating power without the acoustic disruption of traditional turbines—ideal for a commercial district environment.
